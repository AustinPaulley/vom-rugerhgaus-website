Training At Vom Rugerhaus

My specialized programs focus on puppy socialization, behavior modification, and advanced obedience. Whether you’re laying the foundation for a confident, well-mannered puppy, correcting unwanted habits, or fine-tuning obedience cues, every session is personalized to your dog’s temperament and your goals.

I also offer boarding & training, giving your companion a safe, supervised environment to learn and grow while you’re away. Your dog receives consistent, hands-on instruction—bringing out the very best in every canine.
